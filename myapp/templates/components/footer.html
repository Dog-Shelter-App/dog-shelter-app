Pay Sally

PaySally is an independent, nonprofit think tank that researches the impact of economic trends and policies on working people in the United States. PaySally’s research helps policymakers, opinion leaders, advocates, journalists, and the public understand the bread-and-butter issues affecting ordinary Americans.
